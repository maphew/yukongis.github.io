Yukon NTS Index

Posted on 2020-Nov-12 by matt

A commonly used dataset that’s not readily for available for download in ways I find useful is the Canadian National Topographic Series index for the Yukon. Let’s make that available in a variety of easy to utilize formats.

(((post is being updated with new formats as I make them)))

Yukon 1:250,000 National Topographic Series arranged in same order as they appear in the map index. For those times when you need plain text, but still want a graphic-like presentation.
