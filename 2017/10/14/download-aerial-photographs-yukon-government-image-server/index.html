Download Aerial Photographs from the Yukon Government image server

Posted on 2017-Oct-14 by matt
  1. Go to the GeoYukon portal web app,
  2. Turn off all Operational Layers (optional),
  3. Turn on Base >> Imagery layers of interest (shows footprints)
  4. Zoom to area of interest,
  5. Use ‘I want to…’ to List All Imagery at Location, click
  6. In search results “Aerial Photographs” section will have [Download this image] links

This process works for the Aerial Photographs and Landsat Satellite Imagery services. All other image services are used under license and not for public distribution.
